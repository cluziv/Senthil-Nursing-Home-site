Our Story

25 Years of Building Trust, One Patient at a Time

From a small nursing home in Madukkarai to a multi-speciality institution — this is the story of Senthil Nursing Home.

The Founder

Dr. C.M. Senthil Kumar

In 1998, Dr. C.M. Senthil Kumar opened the doors of Senthil Nursing Home with a simple conviction: quality healthcare shouldn't require a trip to the city. Trained in Internal Medicine and Diabetology, and later at the Joslin Diabetes Center in the United States, he built SNH around a specialty that was quietly becoming an epidemic in Tamil Nadu — diabetes.

What began as general practice grew, camp by camp and patient by patient, into a dedicated diabetic care center, a respiratory medicine practice, and eventually a full multi-speciality institution — without ever losing its community roots in Madukkarai.

Today, that same philosophy — accessible, rigorous, personal medicine — guides every department at SNH.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Growth

1998

SNH Founded

Dr. C.M. Senthil Kumar establishes Senthil Nursing Home in Madukkarai, offering general medicine to the local community.

2003

Diabetic Care Center Launched

A dedicated diabetology wing is introduced, addressing the rising need for chronic disease management in the region.

2007

Respiratory & Allergy Services Added

Asthma, COPD and allergy care join the specialty list, backed by on-site spirometry.

2010

Homeopathy Wing Introduced

Dr. S. Jayanthy Senthil Kumar joins to lead a dedicated homeopathic care practice alongside conventional medicine.

2013

Diabetes Expo & Community Outreach

SNH screens 900+ residents in its largest community health initiative to date, launching a decade of ongoing camps.

Present

25+ Years, Multi-Speciality Institution

A 25-bed facility with surgical care, diagnostics, and 100+ community camps run — still rooted in Madukkarai.
