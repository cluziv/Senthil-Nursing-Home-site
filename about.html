Our Story

Built on One Family's Commitment to Madukkarai

Since 1998, Senthil Nursing Home has grown from a single-doctor clinic into a trusted multi-speciality institution — without ever losing its community roots.

The Founder's Story

Dr. C.M. Senthil Kumar founded Senthil Nursing Home in 1998 with a simple belief: that world-class medical care should be accessible to every family in Madukkarai, not just those who could travel to the city centre. Trained in Internal Medicine, Diabetology and Respiratory Medicine — including specialised study at the Joslin Diabetes Center in the USA — he built a practice around long-term relationships with patients rather than transactional visits.

Over 25 years, that single clinic has expanded into a 25-bed nursing home offering surgical care, diagnostics, pediatrics and community outreach — while Dr. Senthil Kumar's recognition, including the Best Doctor Award in 2015, reflects a career spent close to the people he serves.

Our Journey

1998

The Founding

Dr. C.M. Senthil Kumar establishes Senthil Nursing Home as a general and internal medicine clinic in Madukkarai.

2003

Diabetology Focus Begins

Dedicated diabetes management services launched after specialised training at the Joslin Diabetes Center, USA.

2007

Respiratory Care Expansion

Asthma, allergy and COPD care added following advanced certification in TB & chest diseases.

2010

Surgical & Inpatient Wing

Operation theatre, labour room and inpatient beds introduced, expanding SNH into a full nursing home.

2014

Community Health Movement

Large-scale outreach begins — including factory health programs at ACC Madukkarai and Revathi Equipments.

Present

25+ Years of Trusted Care

Serving over 10,000 patients with modern diagnostics, homeopathy, and two generations of physician leadership.
