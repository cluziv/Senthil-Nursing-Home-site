FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment for OP consultation?
Walk-ins are welcome, but booking ahead through our appointment form or by phone helps reduce your wait time.
What documents should I bring for a diabetes consultation?
Please bring any previous test reports, current medications, and a recent blood sugar log if you track one.
Does SNH offer emergency services?
Yes, emergency support is available during working hours. For life-threatening emergencies, please call ahead so our team can prepare.
Can I request health packages without a doctor referral?
Yes, our health checkup packages can be booked directly — no referral required.
Is homeopathy offered alongside conventional treatment?
Yes, Dr. Jayanthy works alongside our physicians so you can combine approaches where appropriate.
